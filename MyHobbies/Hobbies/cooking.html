  • Chiken curry:

    Recipe:

    1. 2 Tbsp olive oil.
    2. 1 small yellow onion, chopped (1 cup)
    3. 4 garlic cloves, minced (1 1/2 Tbsp)
    4. 1 Tbsp peeled and minced fresh ginger.
    5. 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth.
    6. 3/4 cup drained canned diced tomatoes or peeled, seeded, diced fresh tomatoes.
    7. Salt
    8. Cayenne pepper, to taste.

  • Mushroom cream soup:

    Recipe:

    1. 90g butter.
    2. 2 medium onions, roughly chopped.
    3. 1 garlic clove, crushed.
    4. 500g mushrooms, finely chopped (chestnut or button mushrooms work well)
    5. 2 tbsp plain flour.
    6. 1l hot chicken stock.
    7. 1 bay leaf.
    8. 4 tbsp single cream.
